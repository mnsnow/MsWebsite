Night Walker Overview:

Night Walkers are Cygnus Knights that serve... well... the night, obviously. These devious tricksters are lucky and dexterous and have mastered the art of the throwing star. They have a communion with Umbra, the Spirit of Darkness, and use the powers of the night to aid them. When Umbra is unable to heed a Night Walker's call, these sneaky rogues can use poison to stop enemies dead in their tracks. Emo? Maybe. But never underestimate a Night Walker. When the sun sets, you'll never see them coming.
