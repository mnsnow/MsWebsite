Wild Hunter Overview:

The Wild Hunter is a member of the Resistance, and fights for the liberation of Edelstein, her hometown, currently occupied by the Black Wings. The Wild Hunter has a unique relationship with wildlife, and has trained a fierce jaguar to be her war mount. Pouncing into battle atop a giant cat, the Wild Hunter strikes fear into the hearts of her enemies as she peppers them with arrow volleys. However, Wild Hunters aren't given jaguars as pets; they have to earn the right to ride one. In order to access most of the Wild Hunter's skills, you'll have to track and tame a jaguar yourself.
